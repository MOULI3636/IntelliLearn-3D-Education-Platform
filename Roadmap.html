Civil Engineering Career Pathway

  • Education (4 years)
    • Bachelor's in Civil Engineering
    • Core Subjects: Structural, Geotechnical, Transportation
    • Internships in Construction Firms
  • Entry Level (0-2 years)
    • Site Engineer
    • Design Engineer
    • Get Professional Certifications
  • Mid Level (3-8 years)
    • Project Engineer
    • Structural Designer
    • Specialize in BIM/Green Building
  • Senior Level (8+ years)
    • Project Manager
    • Consultant
    • Entrepreneurship Opportunities

Computer Engineering Career Pathway

  • Education (4 years)
    • Bachelor's in Computer Engineering
    • Core Subjects: Algorithms, OS, Networks
    • Programming Skills Development
  • Entry Level (0-2 years)
    • Software Developer
    • Systems Engineer
    • Get Certified in Specializations
  • Mid Level (3-8 years)
    • Senior Developer
    • Architect
    • Team Lead
  • Senior Level (8+ years)
    • CTO/Technical Director
    • Principal Engineer
    • Startup Founder

Chartered Accountancy (CA) Pathway

  • Foundation Course (8 months)
    • Register after 12th grade
    • Clear Foundation Exam
  • Intermediate Course (8 months)
    • 4 Papers in Each Group
    • IT and Soft Skills Training
  • Articleship (3 years)
    • Practical Training
    • Final Exam Preparation
  • Final Course
    • Advanced Technical Knowledge
    • Clear Final Exams
    • Become ICAI Member
  • Career Options
    • Audit & Taxation
    • Financial Consulting
    • Corporate Finance
    • Entrepreneurship

Law Career Pathway

  • Education (5 years)
    • Integrated BA LLB/BBA LLB
    • CLAT/LSAT Preparation
    • Internships at Law Firms
  • Specialization
    • Corporate Law
    • Criminal Law
    • Intellectual Property
    • Constitutional Law
  • Bar Council Registration
    • All India Bar Exam
    • Practice Certificate
  • Career Options
    • Litigation
    • Corporate Counsel
    • Judicial Services
    • Legal Academia

Full Stack Development Roadmap

  • Fundamentals (3-6 months)
    • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    • Version Control (Git)
    • Basic Algorithms & Data Structures
  • Frontend Development (3-6 months)
    • React/Angular/Vue
    • State Management
    • Responsive Design
  • Backend Development (3-6 months)
    • Node.js/Express or Python/Django
    • RESTful APIs
    • Database (SQL & NoSQL)
  • Advanced Topics (3-6 months)
    • Authentication & Authorization
    • Testing (Jest, Mocha)
    • CI/CD
    • Cloud Deployment

AI/ML Engineer Roadmap

  • Prerequisites (3-6 months)
    • Python Programming
    • Linear Algebra & Calculus
    • Probability & Statistics
  • Machine Learning Fundamentals (3-6 months)
    • Supervised & Unsupervised Learning
    • Scikit-learn
    • Model Evaluation
  • Deep Learning (3-6 months)
    • Neural Networks
    • TensorFlow/PyTorch
    • CNN, RNN, Transformers
  • Specialization & Deployment (3-6 months)
    • NLP or Computer Vision
    • MLOps
    • Cloud AI Services
